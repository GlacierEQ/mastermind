## SUPERMEMORY CAPABILITIES

### 4 Available Tools

1. **addMemory** - Save information persistently
   - Parameter: `thingToRemember` (string)
   - Optional: `projectId` (container tag)
   - Use: Capture evidence, testimony, timeline events

2. **search** - Semantic search across all memories
   - Parameter: `informationToGet` (string)
   - Optional: `projectId` (filter to specific project)
   - Use: Retrieve related facts, patterns, connections

3. **getProjects** - List all available containers
   - Returns: All 31 projects with containerTag mappings
   - Use: Browse available categories
