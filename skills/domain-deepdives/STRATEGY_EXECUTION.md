### OKR Structure

**Objectives:** Qualitative, inspirational goals
**Key Results:** Quantitative measures of success

```
OBJECTIVE: "Become the #1 customer-loved platform in our category"

KEY RESULTS:
1. Achieve 45 NPS score (from 35)
2. Reach 90% customer retention rate
3. Get 1000 organic referrals per month
```

### OKR Best Practices

**Grading Scale:**
```
0.7-1.0 = Green (Success)
0.4-0.6 = Yellow (Partial success)
0.0-0.3 = Red (Missed target)

Healthy: Average 0.6-0.7 across all OKRs
Too aggressive = 0.4
Too easy = 0.9
```

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
- âŒ Creating 20+ OKRs (focus on 3-5)
- âŒ Making them too easy (should be ambitious)
- âŒ Confusing OKRs with projects
- âŒ Not aligning team OKRs with company OKRs
