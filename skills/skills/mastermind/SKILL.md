# Mastermind Protocol (GlacierEQ/mastermind)
AI-Powered Development & Specialized Agent System for high-speed generation and forensic analysis.

## Overview
The Mastermind Protocol utilizes 9 specialized agents to orchestrate complex tasks, from microservice generation to advanced digital forensics.

## Specialized Agents
1. **Forensic Analyst**: Deep scan and artifact extraction.
2. **Legal Automation**: Compliance and regulatory reporting.
3. **Device Repair**: Diagnostic routines for hardware/firmware.
4. **Malware Detection**: Pattern matching and behavioral analysis.
5. **Data Recovery**: File carving and integrity restoration.
6. **Chain of Custody**: Audit logging and proof of integrity.
7. **Adversarial Analysis**: Red-team simulation and vulnerability discovery.
8. **Documentation**: Automated generation of technical and executive reports.
9. **Integration Orchestrator**: Multi-agent synchronization and workflow management.

## Capabilities
- **Rapid Generation**: Targeted 45-minute microservice production.
- **DFIR Integration**: Deep hooks into forensic tools and methodologies.
- **Orchestrated Workflows**: Sequential and parallel agent execution.
